About CS Theory

Computer Science Theory focuses on the fundamental principles of computation, algorithms, and data structures, exploring the limits of what can be computed and the efficiency of algorithms. Our faculty and students engage in cutting-edge research in areas such as computational theory, machine learning, and quantum computing.
